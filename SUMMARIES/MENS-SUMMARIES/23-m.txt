THE MYSTERIOUS MURDER, OR, THE USURPER OF NAPLES. AN ORIGINAL ROMANCE. TO WHICH IS PREFIXED THE NOCTURNAL ASSASSIN, OR, SPANISH JEALOUSY

Both stories dwell upon incest, imprisonment, and badly decomposed corpses. In The Mysterious Murder, a girl is locked up in an attempt to force her into marriage, but the intended groom is discovered, just in time, to be her father. In The Nocturnal Assassin, a young man is imprisoned by his wicked uncle; the latter desires the hero's sweetheart and is discovered to have raped and murdered the hero's mother and murdered his father.
